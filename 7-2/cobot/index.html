What is Collaborative Robot?

Flexibility

The flexibility of collaborative robots is mainly reflected in the flexibility of programming. Today’s desktop collaborative robots can be programmed by recording tracks. Collaborative robots can reduce the cost of line retrofits. This is very attractive for small and medium-sized businesses.

Safety

Collaborative robots are equipped with a large number of sensors.  The robot can know whether the person has carried out dangerous operations through the sensor, and thus fully guarantees the safety of people and the safety of production.

Efficiency

The working characteristics of robots are often repetitive, fast, and high-intensity, while people are good at fuzzy cognition.  The efficiency of human-robot collaboration is much higher than the efficiency of people and robots working separately.

In this project, our focus is on large or medium-sized robotic arms for industrial use. Large robotic arms are several times faster and more powerful than small robotic arms, and the hazard is higher than that of small robots. In order to cooperate with such industrial robots, the transmission of data must be fast, accurate and without delay. The collaboration must be safe and user-friendly

Research - Wearable Device and Sensor

We collected and summarized a large number of wearable devices on the market, systematically researched according to location, sensor type, cable, power supply, detection data type, etc., and made a matrix. This allows us to have a comprehensive understanding of existing wearable technologies and sensors.

Research - Motion Capture Technology

Visual or Non-visual

We abandoned motion capture in the visual way, because there is a certain transmission rate delay in the visual way. Non-visual methods can have higher frequency transmission rates, which can ensure the safety of workers
